SOCIO-ECONOMIC, NUTRITIONAL STATUS AND DIETARY HABITS OF ADULTS

Age Group: 18–45 Years

Home Results Objectives Contact

Introduction (Highlights)

  • India faces a growing challenge of both undernutrition and rising obesity levels.
  • There is a dietary shift towards processed foods, impacting overall health.
  • Adults aged 18–45 are often neglected in nutrition policies and studies.
  • This study evaluates socio-economic conditions and dietary patterns to suggest effective public health strategies.

Objectives

  • To determine the prevalence of underweight and overweight among adults aged 18–45.
  • To record anthropometric measurements (BMI, weight, height, waist-hip ratio).
  • To assess the nutritional status of the adult population.
  • To evaluate their quality of life and dietary habits.
  • To study the impact of dietary counseling on the population.
