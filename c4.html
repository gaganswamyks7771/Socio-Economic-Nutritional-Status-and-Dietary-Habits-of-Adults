Assessment on Dietary Intake

Number of Meals Per Day

Among 35 respondents, 13 males and 11 females consumed three meals per day. A smaller portion consumed two meals (3 males and 7 females), and only 1 male had a single meal per day.

Food Frequency Patterns

All respondents were vegetarians. Daily consumption of rice was universal. Ragi, wheat, pulses like toor dal, cowpea, and vegetables formed a major part of the diet. Economic constraints influenced the limited intake of fruits, meat, and nuts.
